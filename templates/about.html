MediaCrush

MediaCrush is a brand new, open source website for hosting your media. You can host your images, audio, and video on MediaCrush, totally free - and we're adding support for more kinds of media all the time. There are lots of media hosting websites, though. What makes MediaCrush special?

Privacy

In the age of government deciet and privacy invasion, many people need to take extra measures to defend their privacy. MediaCrush is the only host that cares deeply about your privacy. When you use MediaCrush, you're always using https - so your connection is secure. We store nothing about you when you use our site. Plus, we're completley open about our activities: we publicize reports on everything we do.

Speed

We've invested in hefty servers and lots of bandwidth. We'll serve up your media crazy fast. We also do a bunch of tricks to make it even faster (don't worry, it's all lossless). For instance, GIFs hosted on MediaCrush are, on average, 1500% faster than other services like imgur and minus. We convert them to HTML5 video and they get seriously better speed. See for yourself on the demo page.

Convenience

MediaCrush is simple and easy to use. We don't have any extra junk - we just host media. We do have ads (we've got to keep the servers running somehow), but click here and you'll never see them. MediaCrush also integrates with Windows, Mac, Linux, and mobile devices - check out the details on our apps page. There's also the great API we offer to developers, free of charge.
